December 2022

Issue #1

 
     

Welcome to the very 1st edition of the SSC newsletter.

What a year. The ask of us individually and as a team was immense this year and we certainly stood up and delivered beyond once again. There have been many examples of great teamwork across all areas of the SSC and great support provided by us to each other. But there have been challenges for everyone in developing in person relationships and it continues to impact on our wellness individually and as teams.

We continue to work at finding a better way. I look forward to having more face time with everyone during 2023. Thanks to everyone for your support, commitment and the work you all do day in day out. What we all do in the SSC greatly matters to our business so please remember that in the more challenging days.

I hope everyone enjoys reading this newsletter as a reflection of the great work we do and the significant difference we make to Glanbia and the value that all of us bring to our business.

Thanks everyone.

 


Kieran Rossiter

Head of Shared Services
